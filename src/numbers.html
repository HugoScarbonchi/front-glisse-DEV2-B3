Gladiator Pro Design 10'8

The Gladiator 10'4 is a highly versatile allround paddleboard, perfect for the shorter rider. The 10’4 is recommended for riders under 5'5 with a recommended rider weight of under 75kg (max weight 90kg). This paddleboard offers shorter riders a stable board with no over reach and a board that is easy to carry. It is also great catching a wave as the rails, thickness and construction allow for a stiff ride even in surf.
